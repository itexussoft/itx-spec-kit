# Healthcare — domain delivery brief

## When to use

Fill this in when scoping a feature or plan that touches **PHI, clinical workflows, or interoperability**. It complements the main spec/plan templates from the base preset.

## Context to capture

- **PHI boundaries:** What data is PHI; where it may flow; minimum necessary and purpose limitation.
- **Access control:** Roles, break-glass, session length, and device or network constraints.
- **Audit:** What access and changes must be logged; retention and review expectations.
- **Interoperability:** FHIR resources, versions, and mapping to internal models (ACL / facade).
- **Consent and legal basis:** Patient consent, BAAs, or regional health-privacy rules that apply.
- **Safety:** Clinical decision support boundaries; human-in-the-loop where required.

## Out of scope (explicit)

- List what this change deliberately does **not** cover to avoid scope creep.

## Risks and open questions

- Record unresolved privacy, safety, or interoperability risks to resolve before implementation.
